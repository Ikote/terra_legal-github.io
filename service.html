Wildlife Conservation Awareness

We strive to sensitize the masses about the dangers of poaching and wildlife exploitation, citing the impact to the tourism industry and the laws and policies against this malpractice.

Edifying The Populace

We use workshops and literature on the concerns surrounding mankind to train volunteers in ways directed at achieving nationwide results for the greater good.

Rallying Support

We seek funding for activities concerning nature and wildlife conservation as well as natural resource management.

Policies on nature preservation

Our principles are aimed at using the established laws on environmental conservation to limit the injustice of deforestation among others done to mother nature. Where need be, we suggest and enlighten concerning the vitality of the earth and laws that would better preserve it.
